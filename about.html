About Us

Sabeel-e-Mustafa SAW Foundation works on social welfare and charity projects, to assist the underprivileged in society, through education, health and livelihood programs. Our team of dedicated and knowledgeable leaders and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that our programs and services are of the highest quality and that they meet the needs of our community.

Our Super Team

Meet Our Founding Member

Syed Noman ul Haq is our founding member and is based in Karachi, Pakistan. Currently we are in the process of registering our organisation in Pakistan, however we have several other branches that are of DGR status in countries like Australia, USA, UK, Turkiye and Canada.

Our founder has previously worked as Senior Assignment Editor at Samaa TV and Senior producer of  News & Assignments at BOL. He is now dedicated to working toward a better future for those in need and providing food, clothing, medical care and education to the underprivileged. Using the name of our holy Prophet (Peace Be Upon Him), our founder started this foundation to honor our Islamic duty to those in need.

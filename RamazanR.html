Ramzan Groceries

The Sabeel-e-Mustafa (SAW) Foundation Limited is committed to providing support to those in need during the holy month of Ramazan through our "Ramazan Rashan Pack" program. 

Ramazan is a time of spiritual reflection and generosity, and many families in Pakistan struggle to make ends meet during this month, especially during the current economic situation. Lack of resources can make it difficult for families to provide food for themselves and their loved ones, especially during the month of fasting. Our "Ramazan Rashan Pack" program aims to address this issue by providing food assistance to families in need during the month of Ramazan. 

Each pack contains essential food items such as rice, flour, sugar, cooking oil, and more, enough to last a family for the month of Ramazan.

 The Sabeel-e-Mustafa (SAW) Foundation Limited is dedicated to improving the lives of people in Pakistan, and our "Ramazan Rashan Pack" program is an important part of this mission. 

We are committed to working with local communities and organizations to ensure that our food assistance reaches those in need. Donate now to support our "Ramazan Rashan Pack" program and help provide food assistance to families in need during the month of Ramazan.

 Together, we can make a difference in the lives of those who are facing difficulties.
